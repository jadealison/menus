WHAT IS AN ECO-FRIENDLY HOUSE?

Eco-friendly houses are homes designed and built with the aim of minimizing their
impact on the environment. They use recyclable materials, renewable energies,
and efficient technologies to reduce the consumption of natural resources such as energy
and water, as well as incorporating bioclimatic designs that take advantage of local
conditions to minimize the need for artificial energy.






HOW DID WE MAKE OUR ECO-FRIENDLY HOUSE?

We made our eco-friendly house with recycled cardboard
that we had saved. We divided the work among my
friends and me, and we built our house in parts. First, we cut
and glued the parts of our house into a square shape and painted
them with paints we already had at home. We made the divisions and furniture
with the same recycled cardboard.





WHAT IS THE OBJECTIVE OF THIS PROJECT?

To demonstrate that it is possible to reuse recycled
materials to build an eco-friendly house and reduce waste.
Additionally, to learn the importance of caring for the
environment and adopting eco-friendly practices in our lives.

MATERIALS WE USE

  • Recycled paints
